Berne, IN Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Berne (English: ) is a city within Monroe and Wabash townships, Adams County, Indiana, United States, located 35 miles (56 km) south of Fort Wayne. The population was 4,173 at the 2020 Census. Berne is widely known for its Swiss heritage, architecture and culture, and for its status as the 'Furniture Capital of Indiana.' In 2021, Berne was rated the 'Safest Small City in Indiana.' Bloomberg Businessweek rated Berne the 2nd 'Best Place to Raise Your Kids' in Indiana in 2011.
